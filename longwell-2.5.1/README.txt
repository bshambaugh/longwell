




                                L O N G W E L L





  What is this?
  -------------

  Longwell is a web-based general-purpose faceted browser for RDF data.



  How do I use it?
  ----------------

  Point your browser to 

     http://simile.mit.edu/longwell/ 

  to read Longwell's documentation.
